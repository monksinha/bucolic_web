Bucolic Kailash team

AI-IoT Automated Food-Mining Computers for food producers

We exist to solve the problem of hunger, ensure food security, and sustain the planet by offering the best sustainable returns for our clients and value delivery for our product consumers.

We co-invest our IP stack, technical know-how and machinery in production and marketing of living and packaged fish feeds, bio-fertilisers and into creation and operations of aquaculture, mushroom culture, vermiculture, dairy and indoor hydroponics establishments. These technology-shared and corporate-managed ‘Food mining’ operations yield Green-Gold and community empowerment. A FoodMine is a cooperatively created and owned agricultural establishment.

The Company and The Work

We have completed the POC phase, have recruited, trained the team and have now begun work on our client location to commission our Shrimp Production Unit, Pangus Production Unit, and Red-Worm Live Feed Production Unit. Through the Manipal Regional Development Office, we have planned to offer turn-key aquaculture, mushroom, hydroponics and vermiculture Food-Mining Computers to existing agricultural farms. We rent-out and maintain these computers to farm owner; wherein the mechanical structure is made from locally procured material and bamboo. We have developed a structured training programme to train the farmers to operate these simple to use computers. Our core IT team situated in California, Hyderabad and Manipal take care of the Automation, IoT and AI layer of computation. In reality, the food is grown on the cloud and printed by these food-mining computers.
