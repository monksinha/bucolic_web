Why Bucolic Kailash is Unique

We believe that the Earth is unbiased and is the primary source of all nourishment, wealth and abundance. Company established with the main objective of Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) Driven Farm Aggregation under the Col.pe.fim (™) traded Turn Key Agritech Solutions offerings. There are Biofloc Aquaculture, Vertical Hydroponics, Fungiculture, Vermiculture/Organic Manure Farming Systems that we automate with cloud computing. This is the closest technology can take us to commercially producing food-in-a-laboratory in the year 2020. The Second objective is to use Data Science to organise the organic and sustainable FMCG market by delivering Automated Farm produce through the NaturePond(™) FMCG trade. It will organise a region's farms and connect their produce as FMCG products to the closest markets to the region through the Company's infrastructure and support, will help us achieve our objectives through local-self-sustaining supply chains. Our Systems based Farms are sustainable at its core. Hydroponics uses 97% less water and our Aquaponics design focuses to operate at less than 1% water exchange. The setup of Food Mining Computers and Bamboo Hoop-House Enclosure uses as much natural building material as possible thereby keeping the costs and carbon impact low . A Farm starts with 20% energy drawn from Solar and wind and gradually progresses towards 100% renewable and after 4 years to a carbon sinking farming operational state. When implemented fully in time, the company will bring together food, sustainable energy, technology, business and policy through aggregated high-value Farming, FMCG distribution and AI’s inferences of life-forms.
